About The Fork and Arrow

Hi! we're The Fork and Arrow, a Locally owned and operated breakfast and lunch spot!

Located in the heart of downtown Campbell River, next to Spirit Square, we strive to bring you quality breakfast and lunch at an affordable price

We also host events and do catering. Whether that be your business event, large family gatherings or more, we'd be glad to provide you with an affordable brunch!

Thanks for taking the time to learn about us and what we do, we hope to see you in the restaurant someday!
